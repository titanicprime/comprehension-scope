# Comprehension Scope

Comprehension Scope is a public, minimal, non-ideological protocol for helping people make sense of the world in high-entropy information environments.

It is not a worldview, not a fact-checker, and not a curriculum.

It is a **small set of repeatable questions** that anyone can apply to any claim, source, or situation to preserve their agency and expand their scope of comprehension.

---

## Mission

Restore and expand humanity’s capacity to make sense of the world by providing a universal, lightweight scaffold that:

- reduces cognitive overload without distorting reality  
- strengthens individual agency  
- improves the quality of collective decisions  

---

## The Comprehension Protocol

The core of this project lives in [`protocol.md`](./protocol.md).

It is designed to be:

- **Universal:** usable across cultures, politics, and education levels  
- **Neutral:** no embedded ideology or conclusions  
- **Lightweight:** usable in seconds, not hours  
- **Free:** no accounts, no products, no tracking  

You can:

- read it  
- print it  
- translate it  
- adapt it  
- teach it  

---

## How to use this

1. Start with one claim, one article, one video, one conversation.  
2. Walk through the protocol questions in `protocol.md`.  
3. Notice how your sense of clarity, risk, and agency changes.  
4. Share it with others if it helps.

There is no platform, no app, and no signup.  
This is meant to be infrastructure, not a product.
